Landscape AnalysisMay 6 – May 25

The Landscape analysis and current state review involves the following phases:

  • Strategy & scope definition workshop
  • Knowledge Transfer around Micro Data Library, Open Data Catalogue, Data Deposit, External Open Data and other Data Sources to be identified
  • Literature Review

The outcomes of this Landscape Analysis are:

  • Create context for Workshop Design
  • Provide initial validation of Findings Mapping of final Strategy Report

The analysis and review document will take close consideration of the Data Deposit, Central Internal Catalog, External Open Data and Micro Data Library Catalogs and other data systems and reports around World Bank Group data strategy developed to date. The objective of this document is to provide context for the workshops and provide a clear starting point to learn from what currently works and does not work within various data systems available.
